# The Crew Blueprint — Canonical Documentation

**What this is:** the permanent, rebuildable record of The Crew Blueprint —
a professional training resource for live-event production workers, focused on
real-world job readiness rather than certification.

If you have never seen The Crew Blueprint, you can read this one folder and
understand — and rebuild — it: its mission, who it serves, how the learning is
structured, its authority/safety and legal posture, its course catalog, its
brand, its content pipeline, and its platform plan.

## The big picture (as of 2026-07)

The **foundation is essentially complete** — vision, course architecture,
content workflow, brand, legal philosophy, website shell, plugin architecture,
and the multi-AI content workflow are all decided. The Crew Blueprint is **no
longer blocked by software development.** Delivery is decided: a **gamified,
static, browser-based experience — not an LMS** (client-side progress, no
accounts/DB/backend; see doc 05). The heavy LMS/next-gen-platform path is off the
table.

The **primary work now is content and documentation** — producing canonical
course packets and building this knowledge base and its governance — not
platform engineering.

## Guardrails (this repo is public)

- **No certification claims.** The Crew Blueprint teaches skills and context; it
  does not certify anyone. It aligns to industry standards — it does not confer
  them.
- **Catalog, not paid content.** These docs describe course architecture,
  titles, and scope. Full lessons, answer keys, and assessment banks live in the
  private content repository, not here.
- No income figures, pricing, PII, or secrets.
