# 02 — Services

**Status:** Documented.

## Core services

Live-event production labor for festivals, concerts, and venue events:

- **General production labor** ("hands") — load-ins, load-outs, staging, set changes.
- **Rigging support** — including supervised rigging and climber/rigger work
  (roundslings, shackles, master links, bridle work, angle/tension awareness).
- **Staging** — deck, scenic, and structure assembly assistance.
- **Lighting support** — placement, run, and strike assistance.
- **Audio / video support** — equipment handling and setup support.
- **Equipment handling & safety** — safe movement, staging, and strike of gear.

## Work contexts

- Festivals (including multi-day runs), concerts, and arena/venue events.
- **National travel readiness** — available to travel for runs across the U.S.

## Roles / rate structure

- Roles worked include **hand** and **climber/rigger** (rigger hours tracked
  separately from hand hours).
