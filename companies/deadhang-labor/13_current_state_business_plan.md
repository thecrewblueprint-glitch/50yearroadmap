## 1. Mission (current)

Deadhang Labor LLC provides **safety-first, reliable live-event production labor**
— stagehand, rigging (supervised/qualified), staging, lighting, audio/video
support, and load-in/out — to production companies, venues, and staffing partners.
Today it operates as a **single-operator** business building the foundation to
grow into a team-deploying labor company.

## 2. Current operating model

- **Structure:** Arizona single-member LLC, owner-operated (doc 01).
- **How work comes in:** **direct relationships** and **staffing companies** —
  not (yet) direct major-promoter contracts (doc 03).
- **What's sold:** specialized production labor as an individual technician;
  supervised rigging within qualifications (docs 02, 06).
- **Delivery:** owner performs the work; no roster or dispatch yet.
- **Not yet in place:** team deployment, a contractor roster, invoice factoring,
  or agency-scale operations. These are **vision-plan** items, deliberately ahead
  of today.

## 7. The bridge: current state → vision

Deadhang reaches the vision plan's staffing-agency model in stages, and only when
each gate is genuinely met:

1. **Now — solo foundation.** Legal base done; systems live; documents being
   finalized. *(This plan.)*
2. **Team-ready gate.** IC agreement attorney-reviewed **and** appropriate
   insurance in place → Deadhang can legally and safely engage a first contractor
   (`dh-10`, `dh-9`, `dh-3`).
3. **First crews.** Lead small multi-person jobs; recruit a small trusted roster
   (`dh-3`, `dh-4`).
4. **Labor + coordination.** Repeatable dispatch/coordination; larger contracts.
5. **Vision plan.** The specialized labor-staffing agency described in the
   long-term Vision Business Plan.

Each step is demand-pulled and gated — no scaling ahead of the compliance,
insurance, and safety foundation that makes it responsible.
