## End-to-end workflow

The standard path of a job through the business:

1. **Inquiry** — a producer/staffing company or direct client reaches out (or
   Deadhang applies/responds to a call for crew).
2. **Agreement** — terms set via the client MSA / service agreement, or the
   staffing company's onboarding (W-9 provided; some clients moving from W-2 to
   1099 — see doc 05).
3. **Scheduling** — dates, location, role (hand vs. climber/rigger), travel.
4. **Execution** — perform the work on-site; **track hours by role** (hand vs.
   climber/rigger) and any reimbursable travel/lodging.
5. **Invoicing** — generate a 1099 invoice (see doc 04) with the required fields
   (name, event name, event date, hours, rate, total) and payment terms.
6. **Payment** — receive payment; record it in the finance-tracking system.
7. **Records** — retain the invoice, payment record, and expense/mileage
   documentation for tax and audit purposes.
