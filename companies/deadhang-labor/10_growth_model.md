# 10 — Growth Model

**Status:** Documented (team-deployment specifics are an open decision).

Deadhang's growth is a deliberate, staged path — not a leap.

## Stage A — Solo owner-operator (current)

Earn as a single-member operator, prove the labor model, and put the business
foundation in place (entity, banking, website, invoicing, records, MSA). This is
where Deadhang is today (~60% on the roadmap).

## Stage B — Lead crews

Grow from solo work to **leading crews on larger contracts**:

- Build the **independent-contractor framework** (roadmap `dh-10`).
- Recruit an initial **contractor network** (`dh-3`) and a **communication /
  coordination system** (`dh-4`).
- Complete **safety documentation** (`dh-11`) as a operating baseline for crews.
- Revisit **insurance** (deferred, `dh-9`) — larger contracts may require it.

## Stage C — Labor-staffing model

Evolve into a **labor-staffing-agency-type structure** — sourcing and deploying
crews to clients — once the framework, network, and contracts support it.

> **Open decision (OD-8):** the exact team-deployment / staffing model, gated on
> securing contracts large enough to justify a team.

## How growth ties to the bigger vision

Each stage strengthens the **Labor → Training → Jobs** engine: Deadhang's crews
are trained through **The Crew Blueprint**, find work through **Production /
Festival Atlas**, and are supported by **Contractor Tools** — and the whole
engine ultimately funds and feeds **the Homestead** (the destination). Deadhang
is where that flywheel starts.
