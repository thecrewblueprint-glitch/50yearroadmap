# 04 — Financial Systems

**Status:** Documented (tax detail referenced, not duplicated).

## Invoicing

- **1099 Invoice Generator** — a production-ready, single-file HTML tool:
  - Natural-language parsing to build line items.
  - Automatic IRS mileage calculation.
  - Multiple payment terms (Net 15/30/45/60, Due on Receipt).
  - PDF download with Deadhang branding; browser-based with local persistence.
- Standard invoice fields: name, event name, event date, hours worked, hourly
  rate, total due (plus client-specific requirements where applicable).

## Payment & finance tracking

- An **invoice and finance-tracking system is in place** (invoicing and payment
  tracking are operational).
- Income is consolidated across sources (W-2 and 1099) in a work-income tracker.
  (Figures are private and not stored here.)

## Recordkeeping & retention

- A **record-retention system** is in place: invoices, payment records, and
  expense/mileage/travel documentation are kept for tax and audit purposes.
- Accounting method: **cash basis**; tax reporting on **Schedule C**.

## Taxes (referenced)

Deadhang maintains a dedicated **tax knowledge base in the private `invoicing`
repository** (`knowledge-base/`), including manuals on estimated quarterly tax
planning, self-employment tax, QBI, an S-corp election decision framework,
annual tax strategy, and tax elections/strategic decisions. Strategy decisions
(remain Schedule C / cash basis; no S-corp yet; continue evaluating retirement,
Section 179, bonus depreciation, estimated taxes, future payroll) are recorded in
`governance/DECISION_LOG.md`.
