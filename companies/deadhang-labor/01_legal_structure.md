## Why this structure

Simple, low-overhead, and correct for a solo owner-operator in service labor.
The single-member LLC provides liability separation and a clean business
identity for contracting; Schedule C / cash basis keeps compliance
straightforward at the current scale. Structural changes (e.g. S-corp, payroll)
are tracked as future strategic decisions, not current tasks.
