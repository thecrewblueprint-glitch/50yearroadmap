# 05 — Contracts & Legal

**Status:** Documented (core templates drafted; attorney review pending). See the
document register (doc 12) for the full inventory.

## Client agreements (MSA)

- Deadhang uses a **client MSA / service agreement** as the default contract.
  The stronger MSA draft was chosen as the standard for clearer project
  authorization before taking on larger contracts. **A client MSA template
  exists** in private storage (doc 12); consolidate to one canonical version and
  have an Arizona attorney review before larger engagements.
- Purpose: define scope, authorization, terms, and responsibilities so
  engagements are clear and enforceable.

## W-9 workflow

- Deadhang provides a **W-9** to clients/staffing companies as needed.
- Some clients are transitioning Deadhang's work from **W-2 to 1099**; future
  projects with those clients are invoiced (see doc 03/04).

## Independent-contractor framework (template drafted — legal review pending)

- Roadmap `dh-10`: the legal/operational framework for **engaging independent
  contractors** — the basis for growing from solo work to leading crews (see
  doc 10, Growth Model).
- **A Master Independent Contractor Agreement template already exists** (doc 12):
  a thorough 23-section template covering independent-contractor status, services,
  no-guarantee-of-work, compensation, tax responsibility (1099-NEC, W-9), safety
  compliance, **specialized-work restrictions** (rigging/lifts/electrical only
  where qualified and authorized), tools/insurance, confidentiality/NDA,
  indemnification, limitation of liability, termination, and **Arizona governing
  law**.
- **Remaining:** Arizona attorney review before operational use (the template
  itself flags this), especially for multi-state classification and workers'-comp
  exposure. This is the gating step for `dh-10` — and the higher priority given
  1099-misclassification risk (see the business plan's risk table, doc 12).
