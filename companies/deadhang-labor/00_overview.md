# 00 — Overview

**Status:** Documented.

## What Deadhang Labor LLC is

Deadhang Labor LLC is an owner-operated live-event production labor business. It
provides skilled crew for festivals, concerts, and venue events — production
labor, rigging support, staging, lighting and A/V support, load-ins and
load-outs, set changes, and equipment handling — with national travel
readiness.

## Who it serves

Event producers, production companies, and staffing agencies that need
experienced, safety-minded crew. Work comes both through staffing companies and
through direct engagements. The business presents the LLC as the contracting
entity where appropriate, with the owner as operator/primary contact.

## Current stage

Solo owner-operator, actively generating income through direct contracting and
staffing-company work. The business foundation is largely in place (entity,
banking, website, email, invoicing, records, client agreement). Active build:
an independent-contractor framework and safety documentation, moving toward the
capability to lead crews on larger contracts.

## Where it fits in the bigger picture

Deadhang is the **labor engine** of a larger vision: a self-sustaining
Labor → Training → Jobs system that ultimately supports **the Homestead** — a
community home base for traveling live-event workers. Deadhang proves and funds
the labor model; The Crew Blueprint trains; Production/Festival Atlas surfaces
work; Contractor Tools handle the back office. (See `roadmap.json` and
`governance/CURRENT_STATE.md`.)

## One-line

> Deadhang Labor LLC: reliable, safety-first live-event production labor —
> today a solo operator, built to grow into crews and a labor-staffing model.
