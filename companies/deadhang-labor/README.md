# Deadhang Labor LLC — Canonical Documentation

The single source of truth for Deadhang Labor LLC. The intent (Stage 1): the
business is **reconstructable from this folder alone** — what it is, how it's
structured, what it sells, how it runs, its systems and assets, its contracts,
brand, revenue, growth plan, and risk posture.

_Public-safe: this folder omits income figures, per-client pay, account/EIN
numbers, and personal contact details. Where sensitive detail is essential it is
**referenced** (e.g. the private `invoicing` repo), not reproduced._
