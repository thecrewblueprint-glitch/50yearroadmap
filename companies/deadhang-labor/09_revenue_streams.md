# 09 — Revenue Streams

**Status:** Documented.

## 1. Contract production labor (primary)

The core business: skilled live-event production labor billed by hours/role.

- **Channels:** staffing companies and direct engagements.
- **Classification:** W-2 and 1099, with a transition toward **1099 invoicing**
  for future work with certain clients.
- **Basis for growth:** larger contracts and, eventually, deploying crews
  (see doc 10).

## 2. Print-on-demand merchandise (new)

A print-on-demand clothing line operating **under Deadhang Labor LLC** — **no
new LLC** (decision: `governance/DECISION_LOG.md`). An additional revenue stream
built on Deadhang humor and branding.

- Model: print-on-demand (no held inventory initially).
- Automation: a planned pipeline (`Shirts` repo) so a "make this into a shirt"
  command handles artwork → upload → product → pricing → publish.
- Status: concepts and automation spec started; production not yet live.

## 3. Affirmation calendar (product)

The **365-Day Affirmation Calendar** (see doc 07) — a subscription webcal
product at `deadhanglaborllc.com/affirmations`. A small, low-touch revenue/brand
product.

## Future

- **Team deployment / labor-staffing contracts** — the largest future revenue
  lever, gated on the independent-contractor framework and securing contracts of
  sufficient size (see doc 10; open decision OD-8).

> Revenue **amounts** are private and not stored in this public repo.
