# 05 — Departments

**Status:** Documented (framework established; coverage ~85%).

## Department framework

Research is organized by **department** — the functional areas of live-event
production. Each department is expanded independently via its research packages
and manifests (doc 04), so coverage can grow one department at a time.

## Coverage status

**Substantially complete:**

- Staging
- Rigging
- Lighting
- Audio
- Video / LED
- Power
- Site Operations
- Logistics

**Started, not complete:**

- **Scenic** — begun but not finished. Completing Scenic research is the single
  most-called-out remaining research task (doc 08).

## What "covered" means

A department is considered substantially covered when its research packages exist
in structured form, are tied to manifests the app can load, and correspond to
research reports (checked by validation, doc 06). Coverage is about the depth and
verification of the research corpus behind a department — not just a page
existing.

## Extending coverage

New departments (or deeper coverage of existing ones) are added by producing
structured research packages and wiring their manifests — the same independent,
manifest-driven path used for the eight substantially-complete departments above.
