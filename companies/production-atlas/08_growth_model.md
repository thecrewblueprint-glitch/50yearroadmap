# 08 — Growth Model

**Status:** Documented.

## The strategic position

Production Atlas has **moved beyond defining its architecture.** The vision,
architecture, branching, research methodology, public-safe data model, and
validation are settled. The next phase is to **complete the research corpus,
improve usability, and establish governance** so the repository becomes a
durable, collaborative knowledge system rather than simply a static research
application.

## Remaining active work

- **Complete Scenic department research** (the one started-but-incomplete
  department, doc 05).
- **Continue expanding employer and opportunity datasets.**
- **Finish wiring the normalized date system** into all applicable pages
  (doc 01).
- **Continue repository cleanup and modernization.**
- **Improve mobile usability, filtering, and planning views.**
- **Build governance and collaboration documentation** so future AI contributors
  can continue work consistently (this documentation folder is part of that).

## Long-term direction (decided)

Evolve into the industry's primary research platform for employer discovery,
vendor ecosystems, career planning, department pathways, opportunity mapping, and
regional production intelligence (doc 00).
