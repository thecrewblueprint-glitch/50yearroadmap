# 00 — Overview

**Status:** Documented.

## Vision

Production Atlas is a long-term **intelligence platform for the U.S. live-event
industry.** Its job is to make industry research **structured, searchable, and
actionable.**

## What it is NOT

- **Not a job board.** It does not post individual gigs or broker jobs.
- **Not a dynamic service.** No login, no user accounts, no backend (see doc 01).

It is a research/intelligence layer — the map of the industry — not a
transactional marketplace.

## What it helps users identify

- **Production companies**
- **Festival vendors**
- **Touring companies**
- **Corporate production providers**
- **IATSE pathways**
- **Department-specific career opportunities**
- **Geographic opportunities**
- **Career progression routes**

## Purpose

To turn scattered, hard-to-verify industry knowledge into a structured, searchable
resource a worker can actually use to understand the landscape — who the
employers and vendors are, how departments and career pathways work, and where
opportunity concentrates geographically.

## Long-term direction (already decided)

Production Atlas is intended to become the industry's primary research platform
for:

- Employer discovery
- Vendor ecosystems
- Career planning
- Department pathways
- Opportunity mapping
- Regional production intelligence

## Where it sits in the ecosystem

Within the wider system, Production Atlas is the **discovery / intelligence
layer** — it maps the industry so workers (better prepared via The Crew Blueprint,
sourced and deployed via Deadhang Labor) can find where the work and the career
paths are. It informs the "find the work" side of the loop without becoming a job
board itself.
