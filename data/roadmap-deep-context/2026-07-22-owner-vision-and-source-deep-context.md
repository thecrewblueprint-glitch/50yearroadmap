## What this means for the repo (plain reading)

- **The system exists to catch a live signal.** The owner receives insights
  continually from an inner source; the repo's first job is to **record them
  faithfully so they aren't lost** — daily, as they come.
- **It must go beyond storage.** The mandate is explicit: not an "idea storage
  system," but a machine that turns recorded words into **real things people can
  benefit from.** Storage is the floor, not the ceiling.
- **The chain of hands:** source → owner (receives + words it) → AI/tools (build
  it into working things) → people (who benefit). Each collaborator honors the
  handoff.
- **The standard stays the same.** Turning words into things that reach real
  people is exactly why the guardrails matter — public-safe, true, no overclaiming.
  The discipline is what makes the vision trustworthy enough to hand to a stranger.
