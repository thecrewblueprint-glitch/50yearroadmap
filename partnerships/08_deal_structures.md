# 08 — Deal Structures

**Status:** _stub — framework scoped, content to fill._

**Purpose:** the shapes a partnership can take, and the terms/legal realities to
get right before saying yes.

**Will cover:**
- **Structures by type** — preferred-subcontractor / crew-lead (staffing);
  data-sharing / referral (production & Atlas); co-branded or licensed content
  (training); mission/community MOU (nomadic).
- **The classification reality** — as a *subcontractor* you're being classified
  (lower risk); the moment you bring *others* under Deadhang, the 1099 framework
  (`dh-10`) and insurance (`dh-9`) become live gates. Which structures need which
  gate cleared first.
- **Terms to watch** — exclusivity, payment timing (cash-flow), non-solicitation,
  liability/indemnity, IP (for content deals), scope creep.
- **Start small** — pilot/trial structures before exclusivity or commitment.
- **When to get a lawyer** — anything binding, especially crew engagement and
  content licensing (ties to the attorney review already queued in `dh-10`).

**Connects to:** where an outreach that succeeded (06/07) becomes a real
agreement. Feeds the roadmap gates (`dh-9`/`dh-10`) it depends on.
