# 09 — Risks & Review

**Status:** _stub — framework scoped, content to fill._

**Purpose:** keep partnerships from becoming a new liability, and know whether the
whole effort is actually working.

**Will cover:**
- **Guardrails / risks** — dependency (one partner = one point of failure; aim for
  2+), bandwidth (a solo operator can cultivate only 1–2 at a time), classification
  exposure (`dh-10`), overpromising vs. what Deadhang can deliver today,
  reputation (a bad partner reflects on you).
- **Public-safety** — no confidential partner info in this public repo.
- **Success metrics** — leading indicators (conversations opened, offers sent) and
  lagging ones (steady calls secured, first crew-lead role, first data/content
  deal). Tie to the anchor thesis: has an anchor emerged?
- **Review cadence** — a periodic check (monthly?) against the metrics and the
  sequencing; when to double down, when to drop a track.

**Connects to:** the feedback loop over the whole playbook — it tells you whether
01's strategy is working and what to adjust.
