# Partnership & Landscape Research

**Purpose:** sector-by-sector research into **who has already built a version of
each idea** in the ecosystem — to learn how they did it, decide who's a
**competitor / model / partner**, and feed real leads into the playbook's
targeting (section 04) and the live list (`data/roadmap/connections.md`).

This answers the owner's standing question: *"There are other people with my
ideas — find them, see how they accomplished their versions."*

## How to read a landscape doc

Each doc classifies who it finds into:
- **Direct analog** — someone doing essentially your idea (learn hardest from; may
  compete or may partner/license).
- **Proven model** — the same *pattern* in a different market (validates the
  approach; emulate the mechanics).
- **The field** — established players in the space (often **partners**: they have
  content/credibility you can pair with, or reach you want).
- **Adjacent tech** — tools/tech that could plug in.

Each ends with **strategic implications** (whitespace) and **partner leads**.
