# 03 — Partner Types

**Status:** _stub — framework scoped, content to fill._

**Purpose:** the map of *who* you partner with and *why each side wins* — the
foundation for targeting (04) and deal structures (08).

**Will cover, for each of the four types:**
- **Staffing companies / labor brokers** — solves demand + scaling + insurance;
  they want reliable, low-risk crew. (Track 1.)
- **Production companies / vendors** — solves Production Atlas data + direct work;
  they want dependable labor and useful industry intelligence. (Track 2.)
- **Training orgs** (IATSE locals, ETCP-aligned trainers, Event Safety Alliance,
  tech colleges) — solves Crew Blueprint credibility + distribution; they want
  quality content and workforce pipelines. (Track 3.)
- **Nomadic-movement orgs** (Roadie Clinic, Backline, Workamper, HOWA) — solves
  Homestead credibility + community; shared mission. (Track 4.)

For each: what it solves for you · what they want from you · fit signals · the
first realistic ask.

**Connects to:** 02 says what you offer; this says who wants it. Together they
define the target profile that 04 qualifies against.
