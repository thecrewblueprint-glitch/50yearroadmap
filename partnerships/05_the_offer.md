# 05 — The Offer

**Status:** _stub — framework scoped, content to fill._

**Purpose:** the one-page offer you actually put in front of a partner — the
single most-used artifact in this playbook.

**Will cover:**
- The **one-page offer template** — who Deadhang is, what you deliver, your
  standards (safety-first, ETCP-aligned), travel radius, and what you want.
- How to **tailor it per partner type** (staffing vs production vs training) from
  the value statements in 02.
- The **"specific ask" principle** — the offer ends in one concrete ask, not
  "let's partner" (e.g., "add me to your preferred-crew list").
- What to leave out (rates, aspirational claims, anything you can't deliver now).
- A short version (email-length) and a full version (one page / PDF).

**Connects to:** built from 02 (value) for a target from 04; delivered via 06
(outreach). Keep the actual filled-in offer copy in private storage if it names a
specific partner or terms.
