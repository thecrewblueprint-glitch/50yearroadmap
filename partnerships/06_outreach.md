# 06 — Outreach

**Status:** _stub — framework scoped, content to fill._

**Purpose:** how to actually make contact and get a yes — the motion, not just
the message.

**Will cover:**
- **Channels** — warm intro > existing relationship > direct email > event/in-person;
  which to use for which partner type.
- **The specific ask** (again) — every message ends in one small, concrete request
  that's easy to say yes to.
- **Message templates** — a warm-relationship version, a cold-but-researched
  version, and a follow-up. Short, honest, specific.
- **Follow-up discipline** — cadence, when to stop, how to keep warm without
  pestering.
- **Tracking the touch** — every outreach logged (ties to 07 and
  `connections.md`, as done for The Roadie Clinic).

**Connects to:** delivers the offer (05) to the shortlist (04); every outcome
updates the pipeline (07).
